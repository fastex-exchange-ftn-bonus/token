What is Fasttoken?

Fasttoken (FTN) is the native currency of Bahamut a layer 1, public, EVM-based blockchain solution with a novelty consensus called Proof of Stake and Activity (PoSA). FTN is currently being used as a utility token in multiple online websites including gaming and e-commerce. It is also integrated with 10+ payment providers.
